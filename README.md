## How I work

Product and portfolio management is my primary craft. I treat it as business architecture in practice. A capability model defines what the organisation needs to be able to do. Maturity against that model shows where to invest. Investment priorities drive the portfolio. The portfolio drives delivery. Organisational structure follows capability, not the other way around. Governance is not reporting, it is traceability from sprint outcomes up to portfolio-level decisions with structured escalation paths. Decision rights are a product concern, not a managerial one. Strategy, capability, investment, and execution form a single chain, and the job is to keep the chain intact.

I apply the same patterns to AI products. Capability models become evaluation frameworks. Decision rights become guardrails. Traceability becomes signal from real queries back into the product. Knowledge is infrastructure, not content. Privacy is a structural property of the product, not a policy promise.

## What I am working on

**[AegisAge](https://aegisage.com)** is a privacy-preserving age verification product I am building as a registered venture. The core product constraint is that the party that knows the user's identity never learns their behaviour, and the party that observes their behaviour never learns their identity. Privacy is a structural property of the product, not a policy promise. The system uses credential-based verification with no real-time calls to third parties and no cross-site correlation.

**[AthenaKeep](https://athenakeep.com)** is an AI knowledge platform for small and mid-sized businesses that I am building as a personal venture. Each customer gets a private knowledge base paired with their own AI agent that answers staff questions, surfaces the right document, and retains institutional knowledge as people change roles. Pricing is prepaid and usage-based, dormant accounts cost nothing, and there is no lock-in. The intent is to make the kind of internal AI tooling that previously required a dedicated platform team available to a twenty-person business without one.
